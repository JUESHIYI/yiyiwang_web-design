A Guide To Red Paint

2020

The project is called the guide to red paint. I took the idea of remaking of Antique from Ai Weiwei’s Freedom of speech and changed the context back to 1966 during the period of Cultural Revolution. My investigation started at the local antique market and at my grandma’s house. I also had an interview with my grandma as she experienced that movement. I started to collect objects that were banned at that time, like anything relates to feudalism, capitalism.

One thing I found interesting was that the censorship that that time was not just simply preventing the unwanted things from appearing in front of public; the authority instead brainwashes people, using the propaganda piece which they called the little red book, to ask them overthrow everything besides the ideology relates to communism. It was a crazy riot and many precious cultural relic were damaged by these morons who call themselves the red guards.

So I came ups with the idea of making a forged “little red book” and use a metaphor to reveal this absurd history and the thus critic the blindness and ignorant censorship at that time.
