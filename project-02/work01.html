Antisepsis

2019

A paper AMA created at the RISD pre-college program in July 2019. The project was inspired by the specimens in nature lab. Beyond these beauties, I started to think about these horrible stories behind how humans kill creatures in mother nature and then appreciate their bodies as well as invade the green environment with giant structures. After further developing, I combined elements from modern buildings and patterns from mother nature on a white paper Ama, and then I asked my model to stand in the green background to make a strong contrast showing the weird disharmony
