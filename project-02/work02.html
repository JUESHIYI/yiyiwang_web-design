The Cake

2019

A clay sculpture created based on the theme of social media obsession. By the first glance, it looks like a regular double-layer cake; but when picking up the slices, the “dark” inside is shown: addiction to likes, over-photoshopping, flaunting wealth and voidness. They build up the cake, but deeply concealed by the thick cream and frosting artificially decorated above. Just like many people’s fake social media life, the cake is delicate, fancy and well designed superficially, but actually made of vanity, voidness and illusions.
