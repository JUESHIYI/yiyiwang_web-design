Inflation & Explosion

2019

An installation created to convey the message about fragmented information at present age. Mixing yellowish pieces of newspaper, magazine and book with puffed polystyrene foam and stringing them into an “explosion” shape, I intended to visualize the concept of how information people daily receive is over expanded, fragmented and inflated due to the advances in technology. Colorful pigments are poured over the top representing the richness and variety of information in electronic age. However, the audience would never be able to take away the pieces when walk through them, just like how Inflated and fragmented information is damaging our memory and cognitive function.
